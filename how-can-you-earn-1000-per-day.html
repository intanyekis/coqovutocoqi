3. Affiliate Marketing

The method of earning a commission through the promotion of another person’s or business’s items is known as affiliate marketing. In this marketing, a link will be shared on social media, a blog, or any other website that will direct customers to a specific product.

If the product is purchased using the link by the customer the affiliate merchant will credit you with the sale and pay you the relevant compensation in accordance when a consumer purchases an affiliate product after clicking your link. One can do affiliate marketing by making a website with high-quality content. Some affiliates share products with customers on their social media profiles or YouTube channels.

  • Most big companies offer a good commission to affiliate marketing to popularize their product everywhere.
  • The company has to pay a big sum of money to marketing, but in affiliate marketing they will pay only for the product sale. 
  • The person can work from anywhere
  • No work pressure will be given
  • Need not have any stock or investment and can be done as a part-time job
  • It can be earned as a passive income.

Types of Affiliate Marketing

  • PPS ( Pay Per Sale ) – The product is purchased by the customer through a direct promotion.
  • PPL (Pay Per Lead ) – The person should share the details of the customer to the seller.
  • PPC ( Pay Per Click) – The customer should purchase the product with a link.

There are two types of products in affiliate marketing one is a physical product and the other is a digital product. One should select the right product and the right company then only the promotion can be done effectively.
